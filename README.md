# PortfolYou_AI Public Notebooks

Public research notebooks from the German-speaking quant finance channel PortfolYou_AI.

This repository contains selected notebooks that are intentionally shared with viewers.
It does not mirror the internal research workspace. Raw notes, private sources, drafts,
and unfinished experiments stay private.

## Notebooks

- `BLD-001-daily-reversal-strategy/` - Daily Reversal / Mean Reversion research notebook

## Disclaimer

These notebooks are for educational and research purposes only. They are not investment
advice, trading recommendations, or a request to buy, sell, hold, or short any financial
instrument. Backtests are model-dependent and can differ materially from real trading
results because of transaction costs, slippage, spreads, taxes, liquidity, funding,
shorting constraints, data quality, and execution risk.
